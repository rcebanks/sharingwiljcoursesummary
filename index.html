Part Two

The Product

Sharing Why I Love Jesus: Your Faith Sharing Course is an eight-session, small group course designed to help Christians write and share their personal faith story. It is highly interactive, action-oriented, and focused on practical application.

The Curriculum System

Sharing Why I Love Jesus: Your Faith Sharing Course

Multiple Components. One Integrated System.

Frictionless Deployment

Curriculum Kit

The curriculum kit is a physical package that contains the leader guide, participant workbooks, the faith sharing chart, completion certificates, and access to the online intro videos.
Tone Setting

Session Intro Videos

The intro videos are short videos to set the tone and focus for each session. The course creators, Rachelle Ebanks and Nancy Carol Willis, encourage and direct participants at the start of each session with personal stories and Biblical truths.
Enables Facilitation

Leader Guide

The leader guide provides an activity outline of each session along with timing recommendations, discussion cues, and facilitation support. The guide is written in everyday language, so that all leaders can feel confident running the course.
Provides Practices

Participant Workbook

The participant workbook is where participants complete most of their in-session activities. Some of the workbook activities include role-playing scenarios, while others provide prompts for writing and refining your faith story. There is a wide variety of activities in the workbook, but they are all focused on one thing: helping Christians share their faith confidently.
Promotes Action

Faith Sharing Chart

The faith sharing chart is an 18x24" poster displayed throughout the course. At the end of the second session, the participants set a goal for how many faith sharing conversations they aim to have by the end of the course. The poster is meant to encourage and remind participants that the goal is for them to share their faith outside of the sessions.
Deepens Learning

Study Guide

Sharing Why I Love Jesus, by Nancy Carol Willis, is the Biblical and devotional foundation of the course. Participants are assigned readings from the study guide most weeks and these readings prepare them for their in-session activities and discussions.
