Part Two

The Product

Original Scope vs. Final Delivery

Original Scope Final Delivery
Facilitator Guide
A comprehensive guide for small group leaders.		 Leader Guide
Full leader-facing curriculum guide with session structure, guidance, discussion support, and implementation direction.
Participant Guide
Originally a facilitator-facing tool for tracking participant progress and completion.		 Participant Workbook
Shifted into a participant-facing workbook. A significant expansion from a tracking tool into a full learner resource.
Scenario Cards
Ready-to-use cards for practicing spiritual conversations and faith story sharing.		 Integrated into Workbook & Activities
The standalone card format was removed. Scenario and practice work was folded into workbook and session activities.
Instructional Videos
Short videos demonstrating how to facilitate key activities and model best practices.		 Scripted Intro Videos
Short scripted videos that shape the tone and instructional framing of each session. A significant shift in purpose and production approach.
Boxed Curriculum Kit
A physical kit with all materials needed to run the course.		 Mailer Box / Curriculum Kit
Delivered as the mailer box and curriculum kit files. Replacement physical sample pending. First Vistaprint sample arrived dented.
Faith Sharing Chart (18×24 Poster)
Designed to be posted during sessions to encourage real-world faith conversations.
Completion Certificates
Added to strengthen the commissioning experience and encourage participants as ambassadors.
Video Thumbnails (×8)
Consistent session branding for an elevated YouTube playlist presentation.
Fulfillment Directions
Supports packing, distribution, and consistent kit assembly.
Vendor & Production Specifications
Print and production specs to support future reprints and vendor coordination.
Lifehouse Workshop Materials
Supplemental materials used in real-world testing and implementation at Lifehouse.
